Hello World

The first post on the website

Hello and welcome to personal blog!

The purpose of this website is for me to write down and share with you the things I am wondering about and the things I am actively learning. I am starting this blog for a couple reasons:

  1. I want to learn hugo blog engine as an alternative to WordPress for blogging.

  2. I want to migrate away the content I’ve written over the years from third-party sites like github.

  3. I want to improve my technical communication writing articles.

  4. I miss the old world wide web that I remember in the ’90s. I miss the creativity, the inginutiy, and quirckiness of personal individual websites that you would typically find from individuals before giant websites like facebook came along. In a sense, I hope this website could be a interesting place that you remember from the past.

What sort of topics should you expect?

  • Programming

  • Open-Source Libraries

  • Tutorials

Thank you for visiting and please don’t forget to bookmark this site.
